Founder and our story

In 2007, a remarkable woman named Elizabeth Mumbi had a vision to create a safe haven for vulnerable children who had nowhere to call home. Having grown up in a community where children faced neglect, abandonment, and the struggles of poverty, Elizabeth felt a deep compassion for those suffering in silence. She knew something had to be done to offer these children hope and a chance for a brighter future. Elizabeth, despite facing her own set of challenges, began by opening her heart to the children in need. She started by helping just a few children, providing food, shelter, and emotional support. Her passion and determination were contagious, and soon enough, more children in dire circumstances found their way to her door. The makeshift shelter was humble, but it was built on the pillars of love, care, and security. and this was later called Destiny Child Care Center

Teaching Children to Obey and Know God at Destiny Child Care Center

At Destiny Child Care Center, teaching the kids to obey and know God is done with love, patience, and understanding. The staff guide the children through simple teachings that help them understand the importance of obedience, kindness, and respect for others, reflecting the values that God teaches. Daily prayers, Bible stories, and songs help introduce the children to God's love and the lessons of faith, compassion, and forgiveness. Through these activities, the children learn not just to follow rules but to make choices that reflect God's goodness in their actions. Positive reinforcement is used to encourage good behavior and obedience, while also nurturing their relationship with God, so they grow in both character and faith..

Together for a Future: Ending Child Homelessness, One Step at a Time

.The motto, "Together for a Future: Ending Child Homelessness, One Step at a Time," emphasizes the power of collective action in addressing the issue of child homelessness. It highlights the importance of community effort and collaboration in creating lasting change for children without stable housing. The phrase "One Step at a Time" acknowledges that ending homelessness is a gradual process that requires continuous dedication and small, consistent actions. Ultimately, the motto conveys hope for a better future, where every child has a safe, loving place to call home, and it calls on everyone to play a part in making that vision a reality.
